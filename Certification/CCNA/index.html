Cisco Routing & Switching

Cisco Routing & Switching involves configuring, managing, and optimizing network devices such as routers and switches to ensure efficient data transmission within a network. This includes tasks like designing network topologies, implementing routing protocols, configuring VLANs and inter-VLAN routing, and troubleshooting network issues to maintain high availability and performance. Cisco's comprehensive routing and switching solutions equip professionals with the skills necessary to build and maintain robust networks for businesses of various scales.
